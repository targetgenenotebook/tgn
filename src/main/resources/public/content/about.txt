<p style=margin-top:10px;">Target Gene Notebook allows the curation of persistent, editable, and distributable databases of genomic, biological, chemical, and other data that pertain to the local neighborhood around a gene of interest.</p>
<p>The current version draws heavily on the many harmonized data sources available through Ensembl (1).
<br>
Target Gene Notebook was originally supported and created at Eisai Inc.’s Andover Innovative Medicine Institute in Andover, MA.
<br>
We encourage further development of this resource.</p>
